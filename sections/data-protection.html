Data Protection Policy

Tevroc Soft ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This data protection policy ("Data Protection Policy") outlines our procedures for collecting, using, and protecting personal data.

1. SCOPE

This Data Protection Policy applies to all personal data collected by us through our mobile and web applications (collectively, the "Apps") and related services (collectively, the "Services").

2. DEFINITIONS

In this Data Protection Policy, the following terms have the following meanings:

  • "Personal data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means.
  • "Data subject" means the individual to whom the personal data relates.

3. TYPES OF PERSONAL DATA COLLECTED

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identifying information, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location information, such as IP address and geolocation data.
  • Usage data, such as information about how you use the Apps and Services.

4. PURPOSES OF PROCESSING

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Apps and Services.
  • To improve the Apps and Services.
  • To communicate with you.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. LAWFUL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process personal data on the basis of:

  • Your consent.
  • The performance of a contract.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation.
  • Legitimate interests.

6. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

You have the following rights:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to rectify your personal data.
  • The right to erase your personal data.
  • The right to restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal data.
  • The right to data portability.

7. SECURITY MEASURES

We have implemented the following security measures to protect personal data:

  • Encryption.
  • Firewalls.
  • Access controls.
  • Monitoring.

8. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

In the event of a data breach, we will notify you without undue delay.

9. CROSS-BORDER DATA TRANSFERS

We may transfer personal data to countries outside of South Africa. We will ensure that such transfers are made in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

10. CHANGES TO THIS DATA PROTECTION POLICY

We reserve the right to modify or update this Data Protection Policy at any time, with or without notice. Your continued use of the Apps and Services following the posting of any changes to this Data Protection Policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes.
