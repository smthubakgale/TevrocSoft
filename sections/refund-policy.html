Return and Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

At Tevroc Soft, we strive to deliver high-quality mobile apps that meet our clients' needs. However, we understand that sometimes our apps may not meet your expectations. This Return and Refund Policy outlines the process for requesting a refund or return of our mobile app development services.

2. Refund Policy

We offer a refund policy for our mobile app development services as follows:

  • If you are not satisfied with our app development services, you must first provide us with written notice of the grievances and allow us a reasonable opportunity to correct them.
  • If we are unable to correct the grievances to your satisfaction, you may request a refund within 30 days of delivery of the final app.
  • Refunds will be issued based on the following schedule:
    • 10% refund for minor issues, such as small bugs or minor design flaws.
    • 20% refund for major issues, such as significant bugs, design flaws, or functionality problems.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your refund request.

3. Conditions for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Have paid for our app development services in full
  • Have provided us with written notice of the grievances and allowed us a reasonable opportunity to correct them
  • Have requested a refund within 30 days of delivery of the final app

4. Exceptions

The following situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • If you have approved the final app or any interim deliverables
  • If you have failed to provide necessary information or feedback during the development process
  • If you have breached our Terms of Service or any other agreement with us
  • If you are dissatisfied with the app's performance or functionality due to circumstances beyond our control (e.g. third-party service outages, changes in operating system or hardware requirements)

5. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Provide us with written notice of the grievances and allow us a reasonable opportunity to correct them.
  • If we are unable to correct the grievances to your satisfaction, contact us at smthubakgale@gmail.com to request a refund.
  • Provide a detailed explanation of the reasons for your refund request.
  • Allow us to investigate and respond to your refund request within 5-7 business days.

6. Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel a project, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that we cannot guarantee cancellation of projects that have already been started or completed.
