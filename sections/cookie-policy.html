Cookie Policy

Tevroc Soft ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, maintain, and improve our mobile and web applications (collectively, the "Apps") and related services (collectively, the "Services"). This cookie policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit our Apps or Services. Cookies allow us to recognize your device and store information about your preferences and behavior.

2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Authentication and security: to verify your identity and protect your account.
  • Personalization: to tailor our Apps and Services to your preferences and interests.
  • Analytics and performance: to monitor and analyze the usage and performance of our Apps and Services.
  • Advertising: to deliver targeted and relevant advertisements.
  • Functional: to provide functionality and improve the user experience.

3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are deleted.
  • Third-party cookies: cookies set by third-party services (e.g., analytics, advertising) that we use to enhance our Apps and Services.
  • Strictly necessary cookies: cookies that are essential for the operation of our Apps and Services.
  • Performance cookies: cookies that help us understand how you interact with our Apps and Services.

4. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

You can manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: adjust your browser settings to accept or reject cookies.
  • Cookie management tools: use third-party tools (e.g., cookie blockers, ad blockers) to manage cookies.
  • Opt-out: opt-out of targeted advertising by visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page or the Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out page.

5. COOKIE RETENTION PERIOD

We retain cookies for different periods, depending on their purpose:

  • Session cookies: deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: retained for up to 2 years.
  • Third-party cookies: retained according to the third-party service's cookie policy.
