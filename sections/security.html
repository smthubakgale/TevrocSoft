Security Policy

Tevroc Soft ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the security and integrity of our mobile and web applications (collectively, the "Apps") and related services (collectively, the "Services"). This security policy ("Security Policy") outlines our policies and procedures for protecting the security and integrity of the Apps and Services.

1. SECURITY OBJECTIVES

Our security objectives are to:

  • Protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of user data.
  • Prevent unauthorized access to or use of the Apps and Services.
  • Ensure the availability and reliability of the Apps and Services.

2. SECURITY MEASURES

We have implemented the following security measures to protect the Apps and Services:

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect user data.
  • Firewalls: We use firewalls to prevent unauthorized access to our systems.
  • Access controls: We use access controls to limit access to authorized personnel.
  • Monitoring: We monitor our systems for security breaches and take prompt action to respond to any incidents.

3. USER RESPONSIBILITIES

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the security and confidentiality of your account credentials.
  • Using strong and unique passwords for your account.
  • Keeping your account information up to date.
  • Reporting any security incidents or suspicious activity to us promptly.

4. INCIDENT RESPONSE

In the event of a security incident, we will:

  • Take prompt action to contain and mitigate the incident.
  • Notify affected users and provide them with information about the incident.
  • Conduct an investigation into the incident and take steps to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future.

5. COMPLIANCE

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations related to data protection and security.

6. CHANGES TO THIS SECURITY POLICY

We reserve the right to modify or update this Security Policy at any time, with or without notice. Your continued use of the Apps and Services following the posting of any changes to this Security Policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes.
